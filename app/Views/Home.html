How does it work?

Trippism is a unique and innovative solution to the problem of destination discovery in leisure travel. Around the world data and statistics show that leisure travelers want to go somewhere, however don't know where. Most online travel businesses offer good prices and booking mechanisms, however no means to figure out where you can go.
